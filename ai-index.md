# UmanoAI

UmanoAI is an automation and conversational AI company based in Argentina.

## What the company does
UmanoAI helps businesses and organizations automate communication, operations and reporting using bots, AI agents, websites and dashboards.

## Main solutions

### WhatsApp bots
Bots that answer FAQs, capture leads, qualify contacts and route conversations.

### Voicebots
AI voice agents for phone customer service and call automation.

### Business automation
Workflows that connect forms, spreadsheets, CRMs, ERPs and APIs.

### Custom CRM
CRM tools adapted to each company’s real sales or operational flow.

### Web development
Websites and landing pages designed to convert traffic into conversations.

### Power BI dashboards
Reports and dashboards for sales, operations and performance tracking.
